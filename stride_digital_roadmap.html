The opportunity

Trust you already own, monetised properly

Stride Social has built five years of trust with 30,000+ creators and turned that trust into brand-deal revenue. What the roster has never captured is the recurring, owned revenue sitting behind each creator's own audience relationship: lead magnets, paid challenges, communities, and coaching. This partnership adds that layer under the Stride brand, with the entire build and operation run by Tha Agency.

In one line: Stride lends the roster and the trust; Tha builds, hosts and runs a proven digital-product ladder on top of it; net revenue splits 50/10/40, and nothing scales until an 8-creator pilot proves the numbers.

The rollout

The roadmap, step by step

A staggered, low-risk rollout with a hard decision gate at Month 6. Nothing scales until the pilot proves itself on real revenue.

1

Discovery & agreement

Confirm the Phase 1 pilot creators, share available engagement and affiliate data to sharpen the projections, and sign a simple one-page pilot agreement, with no formal JV required to start.

2
Weeks 3-6

Phase 1 build

Tha builds the lead magnets and front-end offers for the three pilot creators, staggered so each launch gets full attention.

  • Lead magnet designed and connected to list capture
  • Front-end challenge built, priced and tested
  • Launch sequence written for each creator's audience
3
Weeks 7-10

Phase 1 launch

Front-end offers go live. We collect real conversion data and testimonials for each niche, the first hard evidence of what the audience will actually pay for.

  • Front-end offers live across all three creators
  • Real conversion rates captured vs. assumptions
  • Testimonials and proof collected per niche
4
Weeks 11-16

Phase 1 recurring layer

Communities launch on Skool/Whop for the creators that showed real front-end demand, turning one-time buyers into recurring, durable revenue.

5
Month 6 · Decision gate

Pilot review

The whole partnership turns on this point. We assess real revenue against the projections, re-rate the split if needed, and formalise the JV, but only if the pilot has proven itself. If the numbers don't hold, we stop here having risked almost nothing.

  • Actual revenue per creator vs. projected
  • Real conversion and retention rates confirmed
  • Go / no-go decision on Phase 2 and scale
6
Months 7-9

Phase 2 build

Onboard the five mid-tier core-ICP creators and build their lead magnets and front-end offers using the proven Phase 1 templates, faster and cheaper to produce the second time.

7

Phase 2 launch & scale

Phase 2 launches, recurring communities come online, and high-ticket coaching rolls out to the most engaged audiences.

8
Month 12+

Full roster expansion

Apply the proven playbook across Stride's wider mid-tier roster: the same engine, repeated, with the activation motion resourced for scale.

Risk & how we de-risk it

The honest failure modes

Every plan has them. Here are the four that matter most for this one, and exactly how the structure protects against each.

Highest risk

Creator activation

The model lives or dies on creators actually showing up to record and promote. We mitigate with staggered builds, a launch engine run entirely by Tha, and Stride's own influence driving participation, and we name a resourced activation motion before scaling past the pilot.

Financial

The numbers don't hold

Projections are directional and built on benchmark conversions. The Month-6 gate tests them against real revenue before a single pound of scale is committed. Worst case, the pilot ends having risked minimal cost and zero lift on Stride's side.

Brand

Audience trust

The follower relationship is the asset, and a weak offer sold to a free audience burns it. Every product is built to deliver a genuine win first, monetise second. No income, earnings or guaranteed-outcome claims in any copy.

Operational

Zero lift on Stride

The entire stack (build, hosting, payments, community management, launches) is run by Tha and funded out of the 40% share. Stride takes on no new headcount, no operational risk and no build cost. The only thing Stride contributes is what it already has.
